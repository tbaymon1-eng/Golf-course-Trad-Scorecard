        How it runs

        Course, schedule & start

        Set date, status (draft / live / closed), and default venue or course when your format uses it. Tournament events also use Starts & assignments next.

        Tournament only

        Starts & assignments

        Shotgun waves or tee times—assign groups to starting holes or clock times.

        Shotgun uses starting-hole waves (1A, 2A, …). Tee Times assign a clock time per team or group.
        Default uses 1A → 2A → 3A ... 18A → 1B → 2B ... Custom lets you define your own order.
        Enter comma-separated hole order. Example: 1A,3A,5A,7A,9A,11A
        Local date and time for the first tee slot.
        Teams share the same clock time until this count is filled, then the next slot adds one interval.
        Currently only hole 1 is supported as a tee start.
        Recomputes tee times for all non-cancelled teams in registration order (oldest first).

        How it’s scored

        Format, handicaps & games

        Competition format and scoring—primarily for golf. Clinics, camps, or general events can leave unused fields at default.

        Used when “9 holes” is selected above.
        If enabled, the leaderboard can show a Leaderboard / Skins toggle.
        Example: 100 = full handicap, 80 = 80%, 60 = 60%

        Additional side games

        Closest-to-the-pin and long-drive contests—set holes when you want them in play.

        Turn Closest to the Pin on or off for this event.
        Comma-separated hole numbers. Example: 5,8,12,17
        Turn Long Drive on or off for this event.
        Comma-separated hole numbers. Example: 2,9,14
