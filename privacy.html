Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Cypresswood Scorecard ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

The scorecard may store golf scores, player names, group names, and tournament data entered by users. This information is used solely to provide score tracking and leaderboard functionality.

Local Storage

The app stores scorecard data locally on your device to preserve your progress. You may clear this data at any time using the Clear Card button or your browser settings.

Firebase / Cloud Data

For tournament play, score data may be transmitted to secure cloud services (Google Firebase) to enable live leaderboard updates. No sensitive personal information is required to use the scorecard.

Advertising & Sponsors

Tournament sponsors may be displayed within the app. Clicking a sponsor may open the sponsor’s external website. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. Data is used only to operate the scorecard and leaderboard features.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the course operator.

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements to the app.
